How it works

Manage beds in 3 simple steps

01

Monitor all beds in real-time

See the status of every bed—available, occupied, or cleaning—on one simple dashboard. No more phone calls or manual spreadsheets.

02

Assign new patients instantly

When a new patient arrives, the system instantly suggests the best available bed based on their needs, ward priority, and staff levels.

03

Automate your staff alerts

The moment a patient is discharged, cleaning staff are automatically alerted. Nurses are notified instantly when the bed is ready.
